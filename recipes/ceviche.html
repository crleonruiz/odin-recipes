Ceviche

Description

Ceviche is a typical dish of Peruvian gastronomy and has been so successful that its flavors have spread to other Latin American countries. There are many versions of ceviche and it is impossible to say that only one is the right one, but in this opportunity we will share with you a Peruvian ceviche recipe that tries to rescue all the traditional aspects of the delicious original Peruvian ceviche. Many people enjoy ceviche or cebiche alone, but you can also accompany it with delicious garnishes. In this case, we have used sweet potato, baby corn and some lettuce leaves for refreshment.

Ingredients

  • 1 pinch of peppe
  • 1 kilogram of fish of your choice
  • 1 onion cut into julienne strips
  • 1/4 cup of chopped culantro leaves
  • 1 yellow chili bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 chopped aji limo (for garnish)
  • 12 lemons
  • 1 pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1 cup fish broth
  • 1 parboiled baby com
  • 1 parboiled sweet potatoe (yam or sweet potato)
  • 1 bunch of lettuce leaves

Steps

  1. The first thing you must do to prepare the easy Peruvian ceviche recipe is to squeeze the 12 lemons to extract their juice, reserve it for later. Then, cut the fish into pieces of approximately 3 centimeters. Place the pieces in the container you are going to use to serve the dish.
  2. Mix the fish with the chopped aji amarillo, the fish broth and the cilantro or culantro. Add the julienned onion and crushed garlic. Let it rest for a few minutes so that it marinates well and soaks up all the flavors.
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste and place the boiled sweet potato, lettuce and boiled baby corn on one side of the plate.
  4. Then, pour the lime juice all over the plate so that it is well covered. Finally, cut a slice of red aji limo bell pepper and place it as decoration. Set aside in the refrigerator to cool, as this is a dish that is served cold. Peruvian ceviche is a dish that acts as an appetizer, so you can accompany it with rice with shrimp or a delicious salmon with shrimp sauce. Ready to eat!
