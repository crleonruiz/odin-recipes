Pizza

Description

Pizza is one of the most iconic dishes of Italian cuisine, loved and enjoyed around the world for its versatility and delicious flavors. Originating from Naples, pizza has evolved into countless varieties, making it impossible to declare a single version as the definitive one. However, this time, we bring you a recipe inspired by the traditional Neapolitan pizza, focusing on its classic aspects: a thin, crispy crust, a rich tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves. Many people enjoy pizza as a meal in itself, but it can also be paired with sides like a fresh Caesar salad, garlic knots, or even a refreshing drink for the perfect balance of flavors.

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Cornmeal

Steps

  1. Mix the dough ingredients together by hand or use a hand-held or stand mixer. Do this in steps as described in the written recipe below
  2. Knead by hand or with your mixer
  3. Place dough into a greased mixing bowl, cover tightly, and set aside to rise for about 90 minutes or overnight in the refrigerator.
  4. Punch down risen dough to release air bubbles. Divide in 2. Roll dough out into a 12-inch circle. Cover and rest as you prep the pizza toppings.
  5. Top with favorite pizza toppings.
  6. Bake pizza at a very high temperature for only about 15 minutes.
